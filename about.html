Hi,
I’m Michelle,

I am curious by nature and care deeply about people and things.

Aspiring to work as a creative, I initially majored in fine arts in college, making paintings and participating in gallery exhibitions. I later shifted my focus to international studies, with conflicting interests in culture and politics. My love for language and culture led me to study in Barcelona for one year. After graduating from university, I spent a year and a half teaching English, formally learning Korean and backpacking throughout Southeast Asia.

Through empathy and vulnerability, my greatest connections and experiences were made. By listening to stories of the people and places I’ve met, I learned the power of storytelling and connection. Stories ultimately inspire action, and I found that through technology and design, I have the power to bring people together by telling stories and solving digital problems.

I am committed to finding digital solutions and understanding how humans and technology interact. By keeping humans at the center of my design process, curiosity and empathy become core values that help guide me as a designer.
